初學資料科學前，你該問的 4 大關鍵問題

🌱 資料科學不是工程師的專利，而是一種用數據解決問題的超能力。
初次踏入這個領域，你可能對「該從哪裡開始？」感到迷惘。其實，問對問題，就能讓後續所有分析事半功倍。以下四大關鍵，請務必在動手前先想清楚！

1. 為什麼這個問題重要？

  • 商業價值：能幫公司提升收入、降低成本？
  • 社會影響：能優化政策、改善民生？
  • 學術／個人興趣：只是想探索某個有趣現象？

👉 提示：明確「Why」，才能決定「要投入多少資源」與「該使用哪些方法」。

2. 資料從哪裡來？

  • 資料來源：網站 API、政府OpenData、問卷調查、網路爬蟲、人工輸入……
  • 品質評估：有沒有缺失值？格式一致嗎？來源可信度如何？

💡 案例：同樣是電商交易資料，若一部分來自手動匯入，可能含有輸入錯誤，需先做嚴格清洗。

3. 資料長什麼樣？

  • 結構化 vs 非結構化：
    • 表格（CSV、SQL）→ 可以直接做特徵工程
    • 文字／圖片／影音 → 需先做 NLP、電腦視覺前處理
  • 維度與樣本：行數（樣本數）、欄位數（特徵數）是否足夠？

🔍 實務技巧：先用 pandas.head()、df.info() 快速了解基本樣貌，再決定要怎麼叫Chatgpt修正。

4. 怎麼衡量分析結果準不準？

  • 選擇適當指標：
    • 分類問題 → 準確率 (Accuracy)、精確率 (Precision)、召回率 (Recall)
    • 回歸問題 → 均方誤差 (MSE)、平均絕對誤差 (MAE)
  • 交叉驗證：用 k-fold 或留一法，確認模型在不同資料切分下的穩定度。

🎯 說明：就像考試有分數，模型也需要「數據」來驗證它的可靠度。

結語 & 下一步

以上四大問題，將成為你所有資料科學專案的 根基。

「問對問題，才能找對答案。」

請忽略它，因為萃取當下能夠使用的資訊，也是資料科學重要的一環

4 Essential Questions Every Data Science Beginner Should Ask

🌱 Data science isn’t just for mathematicians—it’s a superpower for solving real-world problems with data.
If you’re new to the field, you might wonder, “Where do I even start?” The answer is simple: ask the right questions first, and every step that follows will be that much more effective.

1. Why does this question matter?

  • Business Impact: Will this help the company increase revenue or cut costs?
  • Social Value: Can it inform policy changes or improve people’s lives?
  • Curiosity & Research: Is it driven by pure curiosity or academic exploration?

👉 Tip: Clarifying the “why” helps you decide how much time and which methods to invest.

2. Where does the data come from?

  • Sources: Web APIs, surveys, web scraping, manual entry…
  • Quality Check: Are there missing values? Is the format consistent? How reliable is the source?

💡 Example: If half your e-commerce data is manually entered, you may need extra cleaning to catch typos or inconsistencies.

3. What does the data look like?

  • Structured vs. Unstructured:
    • Structured (CSV, SQL): Ready for feature engineering.
    • Unstructured (text, images, audio): Requires NLP or computer vision preprocessing.
  • Dimensions & Samples: Do you have enough rows (samples) and columns (features) to build a reliable model?

🔍 Pro Tip: Start with pandas.head()anddf.info() to quickly get a sense of the dataset, then decide how to ask ChatGPT to help you fix or improve it.

4. How will I measure success?

  • Choose the right metrics:
    • Classification → Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 Score
    • Regression → Mean Squared Error (MSE), Mean Absolute Error (MAE)
  • Cross-Validation: Apply k-fold or leave-one-out to ensure your model performs consistently across different data splits.

🎯 Think of it like an exam score—you need solid metrics to know if your model truly understands the problem.

Next Steps & Preview

These four questions form the foundation of any successful data science project.
