Privacy Policy

SwipeClear (hereinafter referred to as “the App”) is a mobile application developed by aicoconfs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our services.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: aicoconfs@gmail.com

1. Scope and Consent

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android version of the App distributed via the Google Play Store. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Core Features and Local Processing

The App is designed as a local photo organization tool. Key features include photo sorting (swiping), progress tracking, and local gallery management.
Important: Most data processing is performed locally on your device. Your photo content is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud services.

3. Data Collection and Access

3.1 Data Stored Locally (On-Device Only)

The following data is accessed or stored within the App’s private database (Isar) or local storage (SharedPreferences). This data does not leave your device:

  • Media Metadata: Photo Asset IDs, creation dates, and basic library info (via Android MediaStore) to display and sort photos.
  • Interaction History: Records of “viewed” or “sorted” status and timestamps to provide progress tracking.
  • User Preferences: Favorites labels, theme settings, sound toggles, and local usage statistics.

3.2 Third-Party Advertising Data (Data Shared Externally)

The App uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to display advertisements. To serve ads, the SDK may collect:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as the Android Advertising ID (AAID).
  • Technical Data: IP address, network status, and ad interaction data (clicks/views).
  • Recipient: This data is transmitted directly to Google/AdMob servers. Please refer to Google’s Privacy & Terms for more details.

4. Permissions Disclosure

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+): To access your photo library locally.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: For accessing and deleting photos on older Android versions.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required exclusively for loading advertisements via AdMob.

5. Data We DO NOT Collect

We strictly follow a “Privacy by Design” approach. We do not collect:

  • Personal Identifiers (Name, Email, Phone Number, etc.).
  • Real-time Location or GPS data.
  • Biometric data (Camera or Microphone access is not requested).
  • Cloud backups of your actual photo files.

6. Data Retention and Account Deletion

6.1 Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device as long as the App is installed.
  • Deletion: You can remove all local data by “Clearing App Data” in Android Settings or by uninstalling the App.

6.2 Account Deletion

The App does not require account registration. Therefore, we do not store any user accounts on our servers. No formal account deletion request is necessary; simply uninstalling the App removes all associated data.

7. International Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

We respect the rights of users worldwide:

  • EEA Users (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since we do not store your data on our servers, these rights are exercised locally on your device.
  • California Users (CCPA): We do not “sell” your personal information to third parties as defined by the CCPA.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent and believe we have inadvertently accessed data from a child, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
